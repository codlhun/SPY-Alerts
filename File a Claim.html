Start Your Claim

Forms & Resources

Below are the key forms you need to file your VA disability claims. Download the forms and find instructions on how to complete them:

  • VA Form 21-526EZ - Application for Disability Compensation and Related Compensation Benefits
  • VA Form 21-0966 - Intent to File a Claim for Compensation
  • Evidence Needed for Your Disability Claim - Learn more about the documents required for filing your claim.

Step-by-Step Guide to Filing Your Claim

  • Gather your medical records, service records, and other supporting evidence.
  • Complete the relevant VA forms (see above).
  • Submit your claim online through the VA's eBenefits portal or mail the completed forms to the VA.
  • Track your claim status through the eBenefits portal or by contacting your local VA office.

Contact the VA for Assistance

If you need help filing your claim or have any questions, contact the VA:

  • Call the VA Benefits Hotline: 1-800-827-1000
  • Visit the nearest VA Regional Office for in-person assistance.
  • For more information, visit VA.gov.
